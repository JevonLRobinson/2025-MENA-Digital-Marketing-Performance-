# 2025 MENA Digital Marketing Performance Dashboard

A performance analytics dashboard tracking digital marketing KPIs across platforms and time for the Middle East & North Africa (MENA) region in 2025.

---

## Overview

This dashboard provides a full-year view of paid digital marketing performance across six platforms, segmented by season and month. It covers four core metrics: Return on Ad Spend (ROAS), Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), Click-Through Rate (CTR%), and Conversion Rate (CVR%).

---

## Dashboard Sections

### ROAS by Platform and Season

Compares return on ad spend across Google Search, Meta, TikTok, Snapchat, Google Display, and LinkedIn — broken down by Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer.

| Platform       | Fall | Winter | Spring | Summer |
|----------------|------|--------|--------|--------|
| Google Search  | 5.01 | 3.70   | 3.17   | 2.70   |
| Meta           | 0.77 | 0.65   | 0.58   | 0.41   |
| TikTok         | 0.68 | 0.44   | 0.42   | 0.29   |
| Snapchat       | 0.64 | 0.46   | 0.31   | 0.33   |
| Google Display | 0.37 | 0.26   | 0.28   | 0.19   |
| LinkedIn       | 0.14 | 0.11   | 0.09   | 0.08   |
| **Grand Total**| **1.49** | **1.12** | **0.96** | **0.78** |

> Google Search is the clear ROAS leader across all seasons. Fall is the strongest performing season portfolio-wide.

---

### CPA and Spend by Month

Dual-axis chart tracking monthly Cost Per Acquisition (line) against total ad spend (bars). CPA peaks in Q4 alongside spend ramp-up, suggesting increased competition during high-investment months.

---

### CTR% and Impressions by Month

Dual-axis chart showing click-through rate (line) and impression volume (bars) across all 12 months. Impressions scale significantly in Q4 (Oct–Dec), while CTR% remains relatively stable, indicating broad reach campaigns.

---

### CVR% and Sessions by Month

Dual-axis chart showing conversion rate (line) and session volume (bars). CVR% spikes sharply in Q4 — reaching ~28% — while sessions peak in Oct–Nov, pointing to a high-intent audience during that period.

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Google Search dominates ROAS** at 5.01 in Fall — over 6x the next best platform (Meta at 0.77)
- **Fall is the highest-performing season** across all platforms, with a blended ROAS of 1.49
- **Q4 drives both peak spend and peak conversion**, with CVR% spiking to ~28% in late fall
- **LinkedIn underperforms** consistently across all seasons (max ROAS: 0.14), suggesting a poor fit for MENA paid acquisition
- **CPA rises in Q4** despite strong CVR, reflecting higher CPCs during competitive spend periods
